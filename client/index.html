How It Works

Sign Up
Start by signing up for Accently and create an account. You'll be asked to provide some basic information and select your preferred language.
Choose Your Accent
Once you've created your account, you can choose the accent you want to emulate. Accently supports a wide range of accents from around the world, so you can choose the one that suits your needs.
Speak to your device
After that, all you need to do is speak into your device's microphone. Accently will analyze your speech in real-time and will change it right away.
